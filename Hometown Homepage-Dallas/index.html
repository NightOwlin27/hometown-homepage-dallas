Top three activities to do while in Dallas

Take in the environment
Go for a stroll at the Dallas Arboretum; hosting a variety of events all year round.
Go for a night out on the town
Located in the heart of downtown, Deep Ellum is the hot-spot for live music, arts, and pubs.
Learn more about science and history
From dinosaurs to DNA, the Perot Museum offers a wide range of interactive exhibits and hands-on activities.
